About the book

The takeover is quick. The flags go up. The world moves on. Greenland is renamed, rebuilt, and claimed—until the interior refuses to cooperate.

A 100-mile road from Nuuk pushes inland toward future mines. Then workers begin to vanish. Reports refuse to close. Elite units arrive. One disappears. One returns.
